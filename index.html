Who am I?

A Full Stack Web Developer, Game Designer, Musician, Photographer, Gamer, and more...

I am a Full Stack Web Developer coming from a game designer and musical background. Most recently I graduated from Lambda School's Full Stack Web Developer course to expand on my well established Game Design career.

For the last 10 years, I have worked as a Game Designer for companies like Cryptic Studios, Disney Interactive, Gree Intl, and Storm8.

I specialized in content creation and content management and felt like that experience would make me a better coder so I went back to school. After 8 months in Lambda School's Full Stack Web Development program, I am prepared to take on the world of Web Development and will be bringing along my years of experience creating quality content and user experiences.
